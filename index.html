Resort

Mexico has very many resorts you can choose to stay at. I personally like the all inclusive resorts. Many of the resorts have more than 1 pool as well as many resturants within the resort. It is definetely hard to find the right one to stay at! Just make sure you read all the reviews.

Beach

Mexico has some of the worlds most beauitful beaches. A lot of them have crystal blue water. I like when I can be sholder deep in the water and still see my feet at the bottom.
